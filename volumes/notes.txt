Persistent Volumes:
-------------------

- can use pools to claim persistent volume 
- will do binding based on sufficient capacity
- access modes - different types available (how should be mounted)
 - ReadWriteonce
 - ReadWriteMany
 - ReadOnlyMany
 
- Relcaim policy 
  - Retain  
  - Delete 
  - Recycle
